Peng Liu joined Peking University in Fall 2025 as a Ph.D. student.  
He received his M.Sc. in Chinese Medicinal Science from the University of Macau and his B.Sc. in Chinese Materia Medica from Beijing University of Chinese Medicine.  

His research interests center on *in vivo* transparency and tumor immunology, integrating optical and molecular approaches to explore biological mechanisms within living systems.  

Beyond research, Peng enjoys stock trading and classical music.
